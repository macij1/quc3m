Why DADK#

There are various other tools to create and manipulate QUBOs, for instance PyQUBO, the D-Wave SDK, and others. The DADK further extends functionalities on creating and manipulating QUBOs. Some of the most important features are

  • Structured multi-dimensional variables

  • Create and manipulate QUBOs

  • QUBO to Ising

  • BQP format

  • CPU emulation of Digital Annealer (up to 1024 bits)

  • Fast creation of QUBOs

  • … and many more
